Fun Spots:

Burj Khalifa

The tallest tower in the world and one of the top attractions in Dubai!

The Dubai Fountain

This is the world's tallest performing fountain choreographed to music.

The Dubai Mall

Known for its luxury stores and aquarium, you can do just about anything in this mall!

Global Village

This is the first family destination for culture and entertainment.It highlights almost every countries culture while taking guests through an array of journies.

Dubai Frame

A gigantic building that shows both old and new Dubai from its deck.

Atlantis Waterpark

The world's largest waterpark with over 105 slides and attractions!
